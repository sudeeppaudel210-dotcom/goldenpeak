About Us

Hotel Golden Peak Annapurna 11 is a family-run lodge in Tadapani, a popular overnight stop on the Annapurna trekking trails. We’re committed to warm hospitality, honest food, and unforgettable vistas.

  • Ideal for Ghorepani–Ghandruk–ABC route trekkers
  • Sunrise terrace with direct mountain views
  • Trekking info, bag storage & route tips
  • Group-friendly dining and flexible meal times
  • Pony service available
